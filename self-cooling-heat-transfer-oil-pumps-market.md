<p><p>Self-cooling heat transfer oil pumps are used in various applications that require efficient cooling of heat transfer fluids. There are three main types of these pumps available in the market: Type RY, Type BRY, and Type RYF. </p><p>Type RY pumps are designed with a cooling chamber that enables the transfer of heat from the pump's bearings and seals to the circulating oil, thereby preventing overheating. </p><p>Type BRY pumps are similar to Type RY but feature an additional cooling jacket, allowing for enhanced cooling and improved heat transfer efficiency. </p><p>Type RYF pumps are specifically designed for applications with high temperatures and pressures, ensuring optimal cooling and reliable operation.</p></p>
