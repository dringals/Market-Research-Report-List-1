<p><p>Brewster Angle Microscopy (BAM) is a type of microscopy technique that utilizes the principles of Brewster's angle to study samples. This technique is mainly utilized for the analysis of thin films, interfaces, and surface layers.</p><p>The current outlook of the Brewster Angle Microscopy (BAM) market is promising.
