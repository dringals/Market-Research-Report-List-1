<p><p>Hot air plastic welding machines come in two types: manual and automatic. Manual machines require the operator to manually control the welding process, including adjusting the temperature and speed. They are suitable for small-scale or occasional use. On the other hand, automatic machines have pre-programmed settings and are capable of regulating temperature and speed automatically. These machines are more suitable for high-volume or continuous welding applications. The choice between manual and automatic machines depends on the specific needs and volume of the welding task.</p></p>
