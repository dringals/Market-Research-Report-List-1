<p><p>Furniture wood coatings refer to protective finishes applied to wooden furniture to enhance its durability and aesthetics. The market can be categorized into various types: </p><p>1. Solvent-borne coatings are made with organic solvents, providing excellent resistance to scratches and wear. </p><p>2. Waterborne coatings use water as a base, offering low toxicity and fast drying. </p><p>3. Radiation-cured coatings are cured using UV or electron beam, facilitating quick drying and high hardness. </p><p>4. Powder coatings are applied electrostatically and then cured under heat, ensuring a smooth and durable finish. </p><p>5. Other coatings include oil-based, acrylic, and catalyzed coatings, each offering unique features and benefits.</p></p>
