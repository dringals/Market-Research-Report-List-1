<p><p>The ITO Indium Tin Oxide Sensor market consists of two main types: Film-Based ITO Sensor and Glass-Based ITO Sensor. Film-Based ITO Sensors are made by depositing a thin film of ITO on a flexible substrate. These sensors offer benefits such as flexibility, transparency, and lower production costs. On the other hand, Glass-Based ITO Sensors involve coating ITO on a glass substrate. These sensors provide superior electrical conductivity and are commonly used in applications requiring higher durability and stability. Both types play important roles in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
