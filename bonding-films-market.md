<p><ul><li>Thermally Cured</li><li>Pressure Cured</li><li>Others (Chemically Cured and Light cured)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bonding films are used in various industries for adhesive applications. Thermally cured bonding films are activated by heat and are commonly used for applications that require high bonding strength. Pressure cured bonding films are activated by pressure and are suitable for bonding heat-sensitive materials. Chemically cured bonding films are activated by chemical reactions and are often used in applications that require quick and efficient bonding. Light cured bonding films are activated by ultraviolet light and are commonly used in electronics and medical industries. Each type of bonding film offers unique benefits and is tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
