Water emulsion products are a mixture of water and organic binders, offering good adhesion and flexibility. Solvent-based adhesives and sealants use organic solvents for application, providing high bonding strength. Solid-state products are in a solid form and are heated to allow bonding. These market types cater to different applications in various industries, addressing specific adhesive and sealing requirements.</p></p>
