<p><p>Professional studio headphones are categorized into three types based on the design of their ear cups: closed back, semi-open back, and fully open back. Closed back headphones have sealed ear cups, effectively isolating the listener from external noise. Semi-open back models have partially open ear cups, providing a middle ground between isolation and sound leakage. Fully open back headphones have completely open ear cups, delivering the most expansive and natural sound but sacrificing isolation. These different designs cater to different listening preferences and studio environments, allowing professionals to choose the most suitable option for their needs.</p></p>
