<p><p>A Successive Approximation Analog-to-Digital Converter (SAR ADC) is a type of ADC that uses a binary search algorithm to convert analog signals into digital data. The converter then iteratively adjusts the digital code by successively approximating the input voltage until a sufficiently accurate digital representation is obtained.</p><p>The future outlook for the SAR ADC market looks promising.
